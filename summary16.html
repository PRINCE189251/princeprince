The Hobbit follows Bilbo Baggins, a comfort-loving hobbit who is swept into a grand adventure by the wizard Gandalf and a band of dwarves. Tasked with reclaiming the dwarves' homeland and treasure from the dragon Smaug, Bilbo journeys through dark forests, trolls, goblins, and mountains, discovering courage, cleverness, and a deeper sense of self along the way.

A prelude to the Lord of the Rings, The Hobbit blends adventure, humor, and a richly imagined world. It celebrates unlikely heroism, the joy of home, and how even small people can change the course of great events through bravery and heart.

Key Lessons

  • Courage can be found in unexpected places and people.
  • Stepping outside your comfort zone leads to growth and discovery.
  • Resourcefulness and clever thinking often beat brute force.
  • Friendship and loyalty are essential on any long journey.
  • Home and humility remain important, even after adventure.
