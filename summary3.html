Think Like a Monk by Jay Shetty shares the timeless wisdom of monks and how it can be applied in the modern world. Drawing from his experience living as a monk in India, Jay shows how anyone can train their mind to overcome negativity, improve relationships, and live with more purpose.

The book covers three main themes: letting go, growing, and giving. Through practical exercises and reflections, Shetty explains how detachment from material distractions and ego helps in discovering inner peace. He emphasizes the importance of gratitude, service, and daily habits like meditation to cultivate a calm and purposeful life.

Key Lessons

  • Detach from the noise of the world to focus on what truly matters.
  • Purpose comes from service and contribution, not just achievements.
  • Gratitude and kindness are transformative practices for the mind.
  • Monk principles can be applied by anyone, regardless of lifestyle.
